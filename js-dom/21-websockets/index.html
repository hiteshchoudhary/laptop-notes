Section 4 — Binary Data Transfer

Binary is 5-10x more compact than JSON for high-frequency data like stock ticks (Zerodha Kite).

Section 5 — Heartbeat / Ping-Pong

Proxies and firewalls kill idle connections after 30-60s. Heartbeats keep them alive.
